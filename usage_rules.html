Facility Usage Rules

In order to provide efficient services to all our guests, a few simple instructions and regulations have been formulated by the management committee. All guests and hosts are kindly requested to read the following document and strictly adhere to these criteria. This ensures unhindered institutional services.

Guidelines & Management

General Regulations

Institutional Access

The Ramanujan House is an integrated part of TIFR designed exclusively to accommodate short and medium-term official visitors to the Institute. Field station staff visiting the Colaba campus must process allocations through their Faculty member-in-charge.

Tenure Limits

Guesthouse accommodation can be provided for a maximum continuous period of 3 months. Hosts must proactively inform their arriving visitors regarding standard parameters and ensure bill settlement execution prior to final departure.

Arrival & Waitlist Lockout

Bookings for domestic guests close 24 hours after the scheduled check-in time, while international profiles are held for 48 hours. No-shows within these windows result in systematic cancelation, opening rooms to waitlisted candidates.

Timeline Transitions

Standard Check-in time is fixed at 12:00 Noon and Check-out time is sharp at 10:00 AM. Timely compliance is vital to allow housekeeping groups sufficient room preparation cycles for incoming academics.

Critical Stay Protocols
  • Data Validation: Please re-confirm booking horizons at the reception upon arrival. Due to high occupancy, uniform room configurations may not always span your entire stay—a condition made transparent to hosts at allocation.
  • Overstay Constraints: Because the facility functions at peak capacity most days of the year, overstays are absolutely prohibited. If your departure runs behind, clear your room by 10:00 AM, securely transfer luggage to the front desk reception locker, and utilize common areas.
  • Dispute Remediation: For scheduling conflicts, consult your institutional hosts immediately so they can connect with the Guesthouse Officer In-charge. Under no circumstances may guests threaten or abuse support staff, who lack autonomous authority to override allocations. Security forces will handle hostile friction.
  • Visitor Policies: Active residents are strictly forbidden from housing unauthorized step-in guests within private chambers overnight.
Utility & Room Care

Guests must proactively shut off all active air conditioning units, interior lights, ceiling fans, close windows, and lock their doors safely whenever exiting chambers.

Prohibited Substances

The possession or consumption of alcoholic drinks and intoxicating beverages anywhere within the Guesthouse premises is strictly prohibited.

Property Assets

Management assumes no liability for valuable personal items left exposed inside rooms. Please make continuous use of the built-in electronic combination safe lockers provided inside closets.

Continuous Improvement

Suggestions or complaints are highly appreciated. Submissions may be safely dropped off via paper forms at the lobby center desk, or transmitted seamlessly to feedback.gh@tifr.res.in. Opinions remain strictly confidential from desk crew.

Environmental Harmony

Noise Restrictions

Acoustic Restraint

Ramanujan House maintains a quiet, productive atmosphere for resident scholars. Playing media devices, TV structures, or music systems at high volumes is unauthorized at any time, and strictly regulated post 10:00 PM at night.

Corridor Etiquette

Please refrain from congregating, loitering, or holding impromptu social sessions inside structural walkways and access corridors to prevent disturbing adjacent research quarters, especially during late hours.

Military Area Environs

Navy Nagar Regulations

Photography Constraints

As TIFR sits enclosed inside the secure Navy Nagar (Naval) Defense Domain, operations run subject to regulatory guidelines overseen by the Indian Navy. Notably, casual photography is restricted entirely to the interior of the TIFR Housing Complex. It remains strictly illegal on external military streets and open campus facilities.

Identification Protocols

Naval Security guards maintain checkpoints to inspect, verify, or interrogate parties moving within Navy Nagar. Visitors must carry a recognized government photo ID at all times. Additionally, the reception desk issues a temporary guesthouse ID at check-in. Guests must cooperate with gate screenings and display both credentials upon request.
