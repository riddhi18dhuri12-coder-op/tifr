Facility Usage Rules

In order to provide efficient services to all our guests, a few simple instructions and regulations have been formulated by the management committee. All guests and hosts are kindly requested to read the following document and strictly adhere to these criteria[cite: 4].

Guidelines & Management

General Regulations

Eligibility

Institutional Access

The Guesthouse is an integrated part of TIFR designed exclusively to accommodate short and medium-term official visitors to the Institute[cite: 4]. Field station staff visiting the Colaba campus must process allocations through their Faculty member-in-charge[cite: 4].

Allocation Parameters

Tenure Limits

Guesthouse accommodation can be provided for a maximum continuous period of 3 months[cite: 4]. Hosts must proactively inform their arriving visitors regarding standard parameters and ensure bill settlement execution prior to final departure[cite: 4].

System Constraints

Arrival Lockout

Bookings for domestic guests close 24 hours after the scheduled check-in time, while international profiles are held for 48 hours[cite: 4]. No-shows within these windows result in systematic cancellation[cite: 4].

Schedule

Timeline Transitions

Standard Check-in time is fixed at 12:00 Noon and Check-out time is sharp at 10:00 AM[cite: 4]. Timely compliance is vital to allow housekeeping groups sufficient room preparation cycles[cite: 4].

Mandatory Protocols

Critical Stay Protocols

  • Data Validation: Please re-confirm booking horizons at the reception upon arrival[cite: 4]. Due to high occupancy, uniform room configurations may not always span your entire stay[cite: 4].
  • Overstay Constraints: Overstays are strictly prohibited[cite: 4]. If your departure runs behind, clear your room by 10:00 AM and securely transfer luggage to the front desk reception locker[cite: 4].
  • Dispute Remediation: For scheduling conflicts, consult your institutional hosts immediately to connect with the Guesthouse Officer In-charge[cite: 4]. Threatening or abusing support staff is strictly prohibited[cite: 4].
  • Visitor Policies: Active residents are forbidden from housing unauthorized step-in guests within private chambers overnight[cite: 4].
Conservation

Utility & Room Care

Guests must proactively shut off all active air conditioning units, interior lights, ceiling fans, close windows, and lock their doors safely whenever exiting chambers[cite: 4].

Restrictions

Prohibited Substances

The possession or consumption of alcoholic drinks and intoxicating beverages anywhere within the Guesthouse premises is strictly prohibited[cite: 4].

Security

Property Assets

Management assumes no liability for valuable personal items[cite: 4]. Please make continuous use of the built-in electronic combination safe lockers provided inside closets[cite: 4].

Evaluation

Continuous Improvement

Suggestions or complaints are highly appreciated[cite: 4]. Submissions may be dropped off via paper forms at the lobby center desk, or transmitted seamlessly to feedback.gh@tifr.res.in[cite: 4].

Environmental Harmony

Noise Restrictions

Acoustics

Acoustic Restraint

The Guesthouse maintains a quiet, productive atmosphere for resident scholars. Playing media devices, TV structures, or music systems at high volumes is unauthorized at any time, and strictly regulated post 10:00 PM[cite: 4].

Common Space

Corridor Etiquette

Please refrain from congregating, loitering, or holding impromptu social sessions inside structural walkways and access corridors to prevent disturbing adjacent research quarters[cite: 4].

Military Area Environs

Navy Nagar Regulations

Defense Protocol

Photography Constraints

As TIFR sits enclosed inside the secure Navy Nagar Defense Domain, operations run subject to regulatory guidelines overseen by the Indian Navy[cite: 4]. Notably, casual photography is restricted entirely to the interior of the TIFR Housing Complex[cite: 4]. It remains strictly illegal on external military streets[cite: 4].

Verification

Identification Protocols

Naval Security guards maintain checkpoints to inspect or verify parties moving within Navy Nagar[cite: 4]. Visitors must carry a recognized government photo ID at all times[cite: 4]. Guests must cooperate with gate screenings and display credentials upon request[cite: 4].
