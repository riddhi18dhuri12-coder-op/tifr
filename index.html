Stay & Eligibility Protocols

01

The Ramanujan house is part of TIFR and is strictly used to accommodate short and medium term official visitors to the Institute.

02

Field station staff visiting Colaba campus for official work must submit a requisition through their Faculty member-in-charge.

03

Guesthouse accommodations are provided for a maximum continuous period of 3 months.

04

Hosts are required to ensure that their visitors are fully informed about the final settlement of bills prior to departure.

🕒

Timelines & Cancellations

05

Domestic Bookings

Closes 24 hours after the scheduled check-in / arrival time.

06

International Guests

Closes 48 hours after the scheduled check-in / arrival time.

07

If a guest does not arrive during the allocation window, bookings are automatically cancelled and rooms are allotted to waitlisted individuals.

08

Check-in: 12:00 Noon | Check-out: 10:00 AM. Please remain punctual to allow adequate room preparation windows.

🛡️

House Code of Conduct

!

Alcoholic drinks inside the Guesthouse premises are strictly prohibited.

!

Overstaying is not permitted for any reason. If delayed, please store your baggage at the reception and utilize common areas.

09

Guests are not entitled to bring unauthorized visitors to stay with them in their assigned rooms.

10

Please remember to switch off ACs, lights, and fans, and close windows securely whenever leaving the room.

⚓

Navy Nagar & Noise Regulations

11

To preserve our peaceful environment, avoid watching TV or playing loud music at any time, especially after 10:00 PM.

12

Please do not congregate in the open corridors or disturb other guests walking through the main passages at night.
