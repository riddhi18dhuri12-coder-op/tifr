Refined Living at Ramanujan House

To ensure the comfort and security of all our esteemed guests, we request you to follow these professional guidelines during your stay at TIFR.

Guest Guidelines

Official Status

Reserved exclusively for short and medium-term official visitors to the Institute. Field station staff require faculty requisition.

Stay Limits

Maximum continuous stay is strictly limited to 3 months to accommodate rotating academic visitors.

Strict Prohibition

Alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited within the Guesthouse premises. Non-compliance results in immediate cancellation.

Check-in & Cancellation

Standard Check-in is 12:00 PM. Bookings are automatically released 24 hours (Domestic) or 48 hours (International) after the scheduled arrival if no contact is made.

Check-out

Check-out time is 10:00 AM. If delayed, please store luggage at the reception to allow room preparation for arriving guests.

Energy & Conservation

Kindly switch off all AC units, fans, and lights when leaving your room. Help us keep Ramanujan House green.

Local Sensitivity

Located in a Navy Nagar residential area. Please maintain silence, avoid loud TV/music, and do not congregate in corridors after 10:00 PM.

Security

Management is not responsible for loss of valuables. Safe lockers are available—please utilize them for peace of mind.
