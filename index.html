Homage to Genius

Infinite Legacy

The TIFR Guesthouse building in the Colaba Campus is named after the legendary Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan.

It consists of three individual performance tiers of rooms. In addition, the facility provides fully furnished and serviced studios and multi-bedroom apartments on campus for long-term residency options.

Eligibility Matrix: Available strictly for registered TIFR Institute visitors. Bookings must clear the official TIFR Datanet portal via authorized faculty channels.
