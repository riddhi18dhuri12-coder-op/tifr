Contact & Directions

To Reach Us

Ramanujan House is located inside the TIFR housing colony on 2, Homi Bhabha Road, opposite the TIFR campus. To reach us, you can take a taxi and ask the driver to take you to the Navy Nagar bus terminus in Colaba, or take bus no. 3, 11, 125 from CST and bus no. 137 from Churchgate station, and get down at the last stop. Then proceed towards the same direction until you reach the end of the road where TIFR campus is located.
